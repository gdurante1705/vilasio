How To Read This Page
Cycle Positioning
Bitcoin operates on ~4-year cycles driven by halvings. On-chain metrics identify: accumulation, markup, distribution, markdown. MVRV and Cycle Oscillators are your primary tools.
Buy & Hold vs Momentum
For buy & hold, use MVRV, Puell, Cycle Oscillators for deep discounts. For momentum, use STH-MVRV crosses and LTH-MVRV Z-Score. Pi Cycle Top tells when to take profit.
Signal Confluence
No single metric is a signal. Look for confluence: MVRV discount + Puell capitulation + SOPR <0.95 + extreme fear = strong accumulation signal.
Holder Analysis
HODL Waves and Supply LTH/STH reveal who accumulates and distributes. Institutions set trend; retail provides trigger. Net Realized P/L captures aggregate emotion.
