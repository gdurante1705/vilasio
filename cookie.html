What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can be used to enable website functionality, remember preferences, improve user experience, and analyse how visitors use a website.

Cookies may be set directly by the website you are visiting (first-party cookies) or by external services integrated into the website (third-party cookies).

How We Currently Use Cookies

At present, Vilasio does not actively deploy third-party analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels on the Platform.

The website is currently operated without active third-party tracking tools such as Google Analytics or advertising technologies.

However, depending on browser behaviour, hosting infrastructure, or future technical changes, limited technical storage or browser-level processes may still occur as part of normal website delivery and operation.

Future Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

Vilasio may introduce cookies or similar technologies in the future for purposes such as:

  • Website analytics
  • Performance monitoring
  • User preferences
  • Embedded services or third-party content
  • Newsletter or account-related features, if introduced

If such technologies are added in the future, this Cookie Policy will be updated accordingly and, where required by applicable law, appropriate notice or consent mechanisms will be implemented.

Vilasio does not intend to use cookies for personalised advertising or behavioural ad targeting.

Managing Cookies

You can control, disable, or delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Block all cookies
  • Block only third-party cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Receive alerts before cookies are stored

Please note that disabling certain technical cookies or browser storage mechanisms may affect the functionality or availability of some parts of the website if such features are introduced in the future.
