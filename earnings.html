PEAD Scanner
Post-earnings announcement drift — active setups
0 signals

Post-Earnings Announcement Drift (PEAD) is an anomaly where stocks continue drifting in the direction of the earnings surprise for weeks after the announcement. The scanner identifies bullish setups: positive EPS surprise + bullish earnings candle. Setup types: Breakout (small gap, trade above earnings high), Test (moderate gap, wait for retest), Zone Entry (large gap, wait for price to return to pre-gap zone). A setup is invalidated if price breaks below the earnings candle low at any point.
