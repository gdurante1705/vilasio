How to read Earnings Intelligence
PEAD (Post-Earnings Announcement Drift) is the tendency for a stock's price to continue drifting in the direction of the earnings surprise for 30-60 days after the announcement. This scanner identifies only long setups from positive surprises — we never short negative surprises.

Setup Types: Breakout (gap <3%) — price barely gaps, entry on breakout of earnings high. Test (gap 3-8%) — moderate gap, wait for test of earnings high. Zone Entry (gap >8%) — large gap, entry if price returns to pre-gap level.

Filters: Only NYSE & NASDAQ stocks with 2.5B-250B market cap (the PEAD sweet spot). If at least one of Sector ETF or SPX is bullish (above SMA 10 weekly), the trend is confirmed. If both are bearish, the stock still appears but with a ⚠ CAUTION badge — you decide. Only positive earnings surprises where price confirms (actual > estimate).

Risk Management: Entry at earnings candle high, stop at earnings candle low, target at 3:1 reward-to-risk. Hold period: up to 1 quarter (3 months). Gap filled = signal invalidated.
