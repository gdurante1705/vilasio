How to read this page
Insider Trading (Form 4): Corporate insiders (CEO, CFO, directors, 10%+ shareholders) must file SEC Form 4 within 2 business days of any trade. Open market purchases are the strongest signal — insiders spending their own money means high conviction. Option exercises are routine compensation events. A CEO buying $5M+ of their own stock is one of the most reliable bullish indicators.

House Financial Disclosures: Under the STOCK Act (2012), members of Congress must disclose stock trades within 45 days. PTR (Periodic Transaction Report) filings are available as PDFs from the House Clerk. Click "View PDF" to see the actual disclosure document with trade details.

Smart Money Overlap: When a stock shows BOTH a positive earnings surprise (PEAD signal) AND insider buying, it represents maximum conviction. The earnings surprise confirms fundamentals, and the insider buying confirms that those closest to the company believe in the trajectory.
