Methodology

The composite score combines two independent signals at equal weight (50/50).

PUT/CALL RATIO (CBOE)
Uses the 10-day moving average of the CBOE Equity P/CR. A ratio below 0.7 signals bullish sentiment (more calls than puts); above 1.0 signals bearish hedging. Mapped to a 0–100 score where lower PCR = higher score.

POLYMARKET
Aggregates the Yes probability of the highest-volume macro and equity markets (Fed rate decisions, recession odds, S&P 500 targets). The volume-weighted average probability is used directly as the sentiment score.
