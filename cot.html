What is the Z-Score? — It measures how many standard deviations the current Big Players net position is from the 2-year average. A value above +2.0 signals extreme long positioning (potential top / reversal risk). Below −2.0 signals extreme short positioning (potential bottom / reversal opportunity). Between −1.0 and +1.0 is considered neutral — the market is within normal range.
